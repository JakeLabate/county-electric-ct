A family shop that grew into a statewide contractor

Al Cortina Sr. started the company from his home in 1963 and built it on referrals. Six decades later his sons Al Jr. and Michael run a Bridgeport headquarters, a deep bench of foremen, electricians and apprentices, and projects that range from residential service work to 368,000 square foot distribution centers.
